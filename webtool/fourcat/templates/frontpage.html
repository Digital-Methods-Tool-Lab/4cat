What is 4CAT?

4CAT is a software suite for capture and analysis of thread-based platforms, created and run by OILab at the University of Amsterdam as part of the ERC-funded ODYCCEUS project.

It is currently under development. At this time, you can use it to query select boards of 4chan and 8chan.

Updates

  1. 29 Jan 2019 We upgraded our server hardware and rebuilt indexes; the full corpus of posts is now once again available.
  2. 18 Jan 2019 Added 8chan as a data source. The boards pol, leftypol, v and brit will be scraped.
  3. 11 Jan 2019 Added the lgbt board to the 4chan scraper.
