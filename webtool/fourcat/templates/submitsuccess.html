+ Tips

  • Use your methods skills to explore data out there and develop your own projects. Coding is a huge plus.
  • With that, join the data sprints!
  • Approach teachers with research proposals/internships
  • Contact people ‘in the field’ - also outside academia, like the arts and journalism. E.g. what we did with Dimitri Tokmetzis & Transmediale
  • Consider picking research topics that’s going to be of interest
