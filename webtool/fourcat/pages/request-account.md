## Request a 4CAT account

4CAT accounts are available by request. Please send an e-mail to [4cat@oilab.eu](mailto:4cat@oilab.eu) and state your
name, affiliation and what you intend to use 4CAT for. You will then be sent a password you can use to log in to the web
tool.

Requests are checked manually, so unfortunately there may be a slight delay before your request is processed and 
accepted.
